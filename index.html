A year ago, you sent me a friend request and I still smile whenever I think about it. After I accepted it, you messaged me saying, “Akala ko si tita,” because Vera was in my name, and you thought I was your aunt. And here we are now, one year later.

I’m still amazed at how easily we got attached to each other, how quickly you became someone so important in my life. It feels like everything happened so naturally, like we were always meant to find our way to each other.

In this one year, we’ve made so many memories together. We’ve shared laughter, joy, and moments that I will always treasure. But not everything was perfect, we also had fights, misunderstandings, and silent battles that we went through even when we didn’t fully understand each other.

Still, despite everything, we are here. We chose to stay. We became each other’s comfort, someone to lean on when things get heavy, someone to come back to even after distance or silence.

And I just hope that even when we don’t always understand each other, we won’t give up on us. I hope we learn, grow, and choose each other again and again, even on the difficult days.

Happy 1st anniversary, my love. Thank you for staying. Thank you for choosing me. Here’s to more years of memories, growth, love, and us, stronger than ever.

Forever yours, always 💖
